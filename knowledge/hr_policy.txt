Vaayu Employee Leave Policy

Annual Leave:

Full-time employees receive 24 annual leave days per calendar year.

Sick Leave:

Employees receive 12 sick leave days annually.

Carry Forward:

Employees can carry forward a maximum of 10 unused annual leave days.

Approval:

Leave requests require manager approval through the HR portal.

Remote Work:

Employees can work remotely up to 3 days per week with manager approval.
