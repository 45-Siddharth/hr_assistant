# Responsible AI Report

## Fairness

The dataset was reviewed to avoid bias and represent employee policies consistently.

## Privacy

No employee names, IDs, salaries, or confidential information were included.

## Transparency

The assistant clearly states when information is unavailable rather than generating unsupported answers.

## Accountability

Model responses were manually evaluated and compared against expected HR policies before deployment.

## Safety

The model was tested to ensure it does not expose sensitive company information.
